# Skill: Release Readiness

Assess whether a story, phase, or feature is safe to ship.

## Procedure

1. Confirm acceptance criteria are satisfied.
2. Confirm relevant tests, build, typecheck, and lint ran or were explicitly unavailable.
3. Identify user-visible changes.
4. Identify deployment, migration, configuration, and operational risk.
5. Confirm rollback plan and known issues.
6. Confirm security review exists when sensitive areas are touched.
7. Recommend go, go with caution, or no-go.
