# Skill: Acceptance Criteria

Convert goals into testable acceptance criteria.

## Procedure

1. Identify the user-visible or system-visible outcome.
2. Define what must be true when the work is complete.
3. Define what is explicitly out of scope.
4. Write criteria as observable checks, not vague intentions.
5. Include negative cases, edge cases, and permissions/data constraints when relevant.
6. Link each criterion to a verification method.

## Good criteria

- Specific
- Observable
- Testable
- Bounded to one story
- Free of hidden product decisions

## Avoid

- "Improve UX"
- "Make it better"
- "Handle everything"
- "Build the full app"
- Unbounded criteria that require multiple unrelated stories

## Output

A checklist that can be verified by tests, build/typecheck/lint, manual QA, or explicit review.
